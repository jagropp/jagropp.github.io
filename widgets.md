## Widgets

Interactive models and tools for isotope geochemistry and microbiology.

---

#### [Rayleigh Isotopic Fractionation](rayleigh_interactive.html){:target="_blank"}
An interactive model demonstrating both classical and branched Rayleigh isotopic fractionation. 

* **Classical Rayleigh**: Describes isotopic evolution in single-product reactions.
* **Branched Rayleigh**: Simulates complex reaction networks where a substrate branches into multiple products (e.g., methylotrophic methanogenesis).
* **Interactive Controls**: Customize substrate initial composition (&delta;<sub>0</sub>), fractionation factors (&alpha; / &epsilon;), and branching fractions (*n*) with live-updating Plotly charts.
