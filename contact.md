### Funding opportunities

Below are some funding programs worth considering for prospective lab members joining the group at the Weizmann Institute. This list isn't exhaustive -- get in touch and we can figure out what fits best.

#### For PhD students

* **[Feinberg Graduate School (FGS) fellowships](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/feinberg/fellowship-aid/phd-students){:target="_blank"}** -- All PhD students admitted to the Weizmann Institute receive a monthly fellowship and full tuition waiver for the duration of their studies; no separate application is needed (interrnational candidates are welcome!).
* **[Azrieli Graduate Studies Fellowship](https://azrielifoundation.org/fellows/graduate/){:target="_blank"}** -- Awarded to outstanding research students in Israel, on top of the standard FGS fellowship.
* **[VATAT (Council for Higher Education) excellence scholarships](https://che.org.il/en/scholarships-outstanding-doctoral-students/){:target="_blank"}** -- Several tracks for outstanding PhD students, including programs for women in hi-tech fields and students from the periphery.

#### For postdoctoral researchers

* **[Weizmann Institute postdoctoral fellowships](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/wsos/fellowship-aid/postdoc-fellowship-opportunities){:target="_blank"}** -- A range of institutional fellowships for incoming postdocs (e.g., Azrieli International Postdoctoral Fellowship, Zuckerman STEM Leadership Program, Fulbright Postdoctoral Fellowship, Dean of Faculty Fellowship, and several country-specific programs).
* **[Sustainability and Energy Research Initiative (SAERI/IES) postdoctoral fellowship](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/SAERI/postdoctoral-fellowships){:target="_blank"}** -- A two-year fellowship for outstanding postdocs from abroad joining a Weizmann group working on sustainability-related research, directly relevant to our work on methane and greenhouse gas-emitting microbes.
* **[Max Planck-Weizmann Postdoctoral Programme](https://www.mpg.de/20566192/max-planck-weizmann-postdoc-programme){:target="_blank"}** -- A prestigious four-year appointment jointly offered by the Max Planck Society and the Weizmann Institute, with time split between a Max Planck Institute and WIS (recommended minimum of one year at each). Requires a joint host at a participating Max Planck Institute.
* **[EMBO Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.embo.org/funding/fellowships-grants-and-career-support/postdoctoral-fellowships/){:target="_blank"}** -- For early-career life scientists moving internationally within two years of their PhD.
* **[HFSP Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.hfsp.org/funding/hfsp-funding/postdoctoral-fellowships){:target="_blank"}** -- For frontier, interdisciplinary life-science research, including cross-disciplinary fellowships for applicants moving into biology from physics, chemistry, or other quantitative fields.
* **[Minerva Fellowship Programme](https://www.minerva.mpg.de/fellowships/fellowship-programme){:target="_blank"}** -- Supports research stays in Israel for postdoctoral scientists from Germany.
* **[Council for Higher Education (VATAT) Excellence Fellowship for International Postdoctoral Researchers](https://che.org.il/en/council-higher-education-israel-academy-science-humanities-excellence-fellowship-program-international-postdoctoral-researchers-call-applications/){:target="_blank"}** -- A national program supporting outstanding international postdocs at Israeli universities and research institutes.

If any of these look like a fit for your background and interests, feel free to reach out before applying so we can discuss the project and host requirements.
